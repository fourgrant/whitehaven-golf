Commish Guide

Step-by-step instructions for running a weekly round from start to finish.

Overview

Whitehaven runs a 9-hole shambles format. Each week follows four steps: set up the round, assign teams, enter scores, then finalize. The active round auto-saves in your browser at every step — safe to close and reopen before finalizing.

1 Round → 2 Teams → 3 Scores → 4 Finalize

⛳ Step 1 — Round Tab

What to do
  1. Set the Date and Course.
  2. Confirm the Buy-in — default is $12 all-in per player.
  3. Click Save Round & Go to Teams.
Buy-in breakdown ($12 default)
Bucket Amount Notes
CTH Pool $2 $1 per par-3 hole (holes 2 & 5)
Main Pool $10 75% team win, 25% skins — or 100% team win if no skins hit

👥 Step 2 — Teams Tab

Check in players

Check the box next to every player who showed up. Only active players appear in this list — manage the roster on the Players tab.

Assign teams

You have two options:

  • Auto-Balance — click ⚖ Auto-Balance to let the app split players into even teams based on scoring averages. Best for most weeks.
  • Manual — use each player's dropdown to assign them to a team letter (A, B, C…). Use this when you want specific pairings.

A live team preview shows the groups as you assign. When everything looks right, click Save Teams & Enter Scores.

Guest players — use the + Add Guest button to add a walk-up player who isn't in the roster. Guests are included in team math for the day but don't affect the permanent stats leaderboard.

🏌️ Step 3 — Scores Tab

Individual scores

Enter each player's total stroke count for the 9 holes in the field next to their name. These are used to update player scoring averages — they do not determine the winning team.

Team shamble score

At the top of each team card there is a gold-bordered box — this is where you enter the team's combined shamble score. The team with the lowest score wins the team pot. Enter this manually (it's the actual shamble result from the course, not calculated from individual scores).

Skins

In the Skins section, use the dropdown for each hole (1–9) to select the hole winner, or leave it as No winner. A skin is only awarded when one player clearly wins the hole outright — ties carry over per your group's rules.

If no skins are claimed at all, the full main pool goes to the winning team instead.

Closest to the Pin (Holes 2 & 5)

Holes 2 and 5 are the par-3s. In the CTH section, pick the player who landed closest to the pin on each hole. Each CTH winner claims $1 × the number of players from the CTH pool.

The payout table updates live as you enter scores, skins, and CTH winners. No need to save — just keep entering data and the numbers will reflect correctly.

💰 Step 4 — Review Payouts & Finalize

Track cash

The payout table shows every player's winnings broken out by team win, skins, and CTH. Use the Paid In and Paid Out checkboxes to track who has settled up as you collect and distribute cash.

Share with the group

Click Copy Payout Summary to copy a plain-text summary to your clipboard — ready to paste into a group chat.

Finalize the round

When all scores and payouts are confirmed, click ✓ Finalize Round. This will:

  • Save the round permanently to the database.
  • Update each player's scoring average (last 10 rounds).
  • Archive the round to the History tab for everyone to view.
  • Clear the active round from your browser.
This cannot be undone. Finalized rounds can be deleted from History, but deletion is permanent and will reverse the stat updates for all players in that round.

🗂 Players Tab — Roster Management

The Players tab is separate from the weekly round flow — use it any time to manage the roster.

  • Add player — type their name and click Add. They'll appear at check-in immediately.
  • Deactivate — players sitting out the season can be deactivated so they don't clutter the check-in list. Their history and averages are preserved.
  • Reactivate — bring them back at any time with one click.

📊 How Scoring Averages Work

  • Each player's average is calculated from their last 10 finalized rounds.
  • New players start with a seeded historical average until they have 10 rounds in the system — set this when adding them if you know their typical score.
  • Auto-Balance uses these averages to create balanced teams, so keeping scores accurate each week matters.
  • Individual scores (entered per player in the Scores tab) feed the averages — the team shamble score does not.

Quick Reference

Question Answer
What if I close the browser mid-round? The active round is auto-saved locally. Reopen the app and continue from where you left off.
What if nobody wins a skin? Leave all skins as No winner. The full main pool rolls to the winning team automatically.
Can I change scores after finalizing? No — delete the round from History and re-enter if a correction is needed. All stat updates reverse on delete.
What's the difference between team score and individual scores? Team score (gold box) is the shamble result — it determines the winner. Individual scores feed each player's personal average.
Which holes are par 3s? Holes 2 and 5. CTH is only contested on these two holes.
How much does each CTH winner get? $1 × number of players checked in, drawn from the $2-per-player CTH pool.
